VisiAge – Fall & Cough Detection System for Elderly People

GitHub Repo

Objective

The goal of this project was to design a comprehensive AI-powered health monitoring system for elderly individuals, capable of detecting falls and coughing episodes in real time — two critical indicators of health risks. The system aimed to improve personal safety and enable timely medical response.

Client & Project Details

This project was carried out for Mediaan, an IT consulting company based in Belgium. The work was structured into two phases:

  • Research Phase – exploring potential solutions, collecting data, and defining the technical approach.
  • Realization Phase – implementing, testing, and integrating the cough and fall detection modules into the system.

The overall project duration was approximately 4 to 5 months.

Team Composition

A cross-disciplinary team of 6 members worked together:

  • 2 members – App Development
  • 2 members – CCS Networking
  • 2 members – Artificial Intelligence (including my role)

Technologies Used

  • Python
  • XGBoost
  • Librosa
  • MFCC
  • scikit-learn
  • CoreAudio
  • iPhone & Laptop Microphones
  • Real-time Audio Streaming

My Contribution – Cough Detection Module

As part of the AI team, I was responsible for building the cough detection system. My tasks included:

  • Collected real-world audio data (coughs and background noise) using iPhone and laptop microphones.
  • Processed audio signals and extracted features with MFCCs (Mel-Frequency Cepstral Coefficients).
  • Fine-tuned a pre-trained XGBoost model for cough classification.
  • Optimized the pipeline for real-time performance and integrated it with the monitoring system.

Results

The module successfully detected cough episodes in real time with high precision, working seamlessly with consumer devices such as iPhones and laptops. This highlighted the system’s potential for cost-effective healthcare monitoring in elderly care environments.

Key Learnings

  • Hands-on experience with real-time audio signal processing using consumer devices.
  • Application of XGBoost in healthcare-related detection tasks.
  • Practical knowledge of MFCC feature extraction for audio classification.
  • Enhanced interdisciplinary collaboration across AI, networking, and app development teams.
  • Balancing accuracy and real-time performance for real-world healthcare deployment.
