VisiAge – Fall & Cough Detection System

An AI-powered health monitoring project designed for elderly care, focused on detecting falls and coughing episodes in real time. The goal was to support faster intervention, improve personal safety, and demonstrate the potential of accessible healthcare technology.

Objective

The goal of this project was to design a comprehensive AI-powered health monitoring system for elderly individuals, capable of detecting falls and coughing episodes in real time, two critical indicators of health risk.

Client & Project Details

This project was carried out for Mediaan, an IT consulting company based in Belgium. The work was structured into two phases:

  • Research Phase — exploring solutions, collecting data, and defining the technical approach
  • Realization Phase — implementing, testing, and integrating the cough and fall detection modules

The overall project duration was approximately four to five months.

Project Overview

My Contribution – Cough Detection Module

My role focused on building the audio-based detection pipeline within the broader health monitoring system.

Applied AI Work

As part of the AI team, I was responsible for designing and implementing the cough detection system, from data collection through to real-time classification.

  • Collected real-world audio samples of coughs and background noise using iPhone and laptop microphones
  • Processed audio signals and extracted MFCC features for classification
  • Fine-tuned a pre-trained XGBoost model for cough detection
  • Optimized the pipeline for real-time responsiveness and system integration

Results

Performance outcome of the implemented cough detection module.

System Outcome

The module successfully detected cough episodes in real time with strong precision while working on accessible consumer devices such as iPhones and laptops. This demonstrated the feasibility of cost-effective healthcare monitoring in elderly care contexts.
