Superstore Sales & Shipping Analytics

A Power BI project focused on analyzing sales performance, profit, and shipping efficiency using a retail dataset. The dashboard supports both strategic and operational decision-making by giving executives, sales teams, and supply chain stakeholders clear and interactive business insights.

Objective

This personal project aimed to build a complete business intelligence solution in Microsoft Power BI to analyze sales, profit, and shipping performance using a real-world retail dataset.

The dashboard was designed to serve multiple audiences, including executives, sales managers, and supply chain teams, while also strengthening skills in analysis, visualization, and dashboard design.

Dataset

Core business data used to build the analytical model.

Retail Dataset

The project used the Superstore dataset, a sample retail dataset containing several years of U.S. sales activity and logistics information.

  • Customer orders with dates, shipping priorities, and delivery times
  • Product categories and subcategories
  • Sales, discounts, quantities, and profit margins
  • Shipping details including modes and delays
  • Geographical data by region, state, and city

Process

Steps followed to prepare the data model and build the dashboard solution.

BI Workflow
  • Cleaned and organized the dataset for analysis
  • Created data models in Power BI
  • Designed dashboard views for executives, sales managers, and supply chain teams
  • Built DAX measures to calculate sales, profit margin, and shipping efficiency
  • Added interactive visuals, drill-through pages, and hierarchies for easier navigation

Results

Business value and analytical outcomes delivered by the final dashboard.

Dashboard Outcome

The final dashboard delivered clear, interactive insights and supported exploration across both strategic and operational questions.

  • Filter data by region, product category, and time period
  • Track executive KPIs such as Total Sales, Profit Margin, and Shipping Cost
  • Combine high-level overview with detailed operational analysis
  • Support better decision-making for both leadership and operational teams

Key Learnings

Skills strengthened through the design and delivery of this Power BI project.

Growth & Skills
  • Preparing and structuring raw business data for analysis
  • Designing dashboards that are both clear and easy to use
  • Working with DAX, hierarchies, bookmarks, and formatting options
  • Building analytical experiences for different types of business users
  • Balancing usability with detailed analytical depth
