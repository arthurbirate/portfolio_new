Coffee Shop Sales & Customer Behavior Analytics

This project analyzes transactional coffee shop sales data to understand revenue performance, product contribution, customer purchasing behavior, and time-based demand patterns. The solution converts transaction-level data into actionable operational and financial insights.

Project Objective

The objective of this project was to:

  • Monitor revenue performance and transaction metrics
  • Identify top-performing and underperforming products
  • Detect seasonality and monthly revenue fluctuations
  • Analyze customer purchasing behavior across time
  • Support operational and staffing optimization

Tools & Technologies

  • Tableau Desktop — dashboards, KPIs, filtering, comparisons
  • Calculated fields — custom metrics & measures
  • Time analysis — hourly, weekday, time-of-day segmentation
  • Heatmaps — peak demand window identification

Dashboards

Two dashboards designed for financial monitoring and time-based customer behavior analysis.

Sales Performance & Revenue Overview Dashboard

Financial Summary

This dashboard provides a structured financial summary of business performance. It tracks total revenue, total transactions, and average transaction value to measure profitability and operational efficiency.

It analyzes product-level revenue contribution to identify key revenue drivers and monthly trends to detect seasonal fluctuations. Payment method distribution further highlights customer purchasing preferences.

Its purpose is to support financial monitoring, pricing strategy evaluation, and product mix optimization.

Customer Behavior & Time Analysis Dashboard

Operational Intelligence

This dashboard focuses on time-based purchasing patterns by analyzing revenue and transactions across time of day, weekday, and hourly intervals.

Through heatmaps and time segmentation, it identifies peak demand windows and product preferences throughout the day. These insights support workforce planning, inventory management, and promotional timing.

Its purpose is to translate transactional timestamps into operational intelligence that improves efficiency and customer experience.
